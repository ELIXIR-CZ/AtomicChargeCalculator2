Atomic Charge Calculator II

Atomic Charge Calculator II (ACC II) is an application for fast calculation of partial atomic charges. It features 17 empirical methods along with parameters from literature. Short introduction covers the basic usage of ACC II.

This example focuses on acid dissociation of three frequently used drugs – paracetamol, warfarin and oxytocin. Their dissociating hydrogens are highlighted here. Apoptotic protein activation

BAX protein regulates an apoptosis process. In our example, we show inactive BAX (PDB ID 1f16) and activated BAX (PDB ID 2k7w). The activation causes a charge redistribution that also includes C domain depolarization (see here). This depolarization causes release of the C domain, which can then penetrate mitochondrial membrane and start the apoptosis (Ionescu et al., Plos Comp. Biol., 2012).
